Meetendow

We are developing an app/web-platform called "MeetEndow"which provides Individual-to-Business and Business-to-Business lending and borrowing. Our goal is to provide a simplified process for borrowers and access to better-value loans for lenders; bringing the two parties together in one streamlined process.

This platform will distribute investments so that investors can match their target returns. MeetEndow will offer short-term financing for maximum of 5 years. Borrowers will receive working capital or equipment purchase loans only after proper verification of financials and other necessary documents. The greatest advantage for the Lenders is that they can have easy access to borrower’s business profile and financial reports which will help them to make necessary decisions before investment

Investment by the lender will be in a range from $500 to $50000. Borrowers may borrow for working capital, business expansion, new equipment purchase and launching something innovative. Investors/Lenders will be able to use the app to generate target returns, see best returns over 3-plus years and find tax saving options. MeetEndow will split investor's investments into small parts, which are then matched with different business loans. The app will work out various ways to distribute investment as to achieve target returns. Lenders will receive monthly repayments, including interest, but minus our loan servicing fee. We factor possible loan defaults when calculating target return. By allowing us re-invest repayments as they come-in, you can earn interest-on-interest.

Mortgage app

We believe that everyone should be able to buy their own home. To reach this goal, we are developing a web platform which will enable home buyers to find their dream home based on monthly payments, rather than real estate price. This web platform will connect home finders and lenders (financial institutions, wealthy individuals or other businesses), so every home buyer can be eligible for home financing. Home financing is a challenge everywhere, especially in big metropolitan centres where the cost to income ratio makes home buying unattainable for many. Our web platform's matching process will enable every home buyer to get easy, low financing. It’s a win-win situation for everyone. Buyers just need to choose their monthly payment, find their home, fill the form, and the rest we will take care. Just 3 easy steps:

1. Choose your own EMI              
2. Choose your own home - House/condo/apartment
3. Fill up the form. We will help you in getting your mortgage based on your EMI.
