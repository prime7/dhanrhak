THE TEAM

Raj Mukherjee

BCOM, PB Finance

I graduated from The University of Calcutta, India.  I have over 7 years of experience running a family business that taught me the complex and agile paths of the business world. As well, I have owned a construction business that focused on commercial buildings. Construction in India is challenging, as one needs to be politically savvy. The skills and know-how that I acquired through successful completion of these projects increased my self confidence and passion for success.

While studying, I started doing day trading, where my biggest trade in marijuana stocks made me a lot of money. My plan since my business school days has always been to do something innovative in finance which will help non-finance people use financial tools better. After completion, I started this fintech company DhanRhak Investments Inc.
